We facilitate our clients to concentrate on building and working on their business instead of engaging unproductively in their business. We offer a collection and delivery service and arrange meetings at the client's site as required.

We ensure clients fully understand the implications of VAT, RCT and other withholding taxes on mark-up calculations and profit margins. Clients are made aware of the various PRSI and Income Tax rates and thresholds when determining staffing levels.

For private tax clients we offer after-hours appointments at the client's home. This has become a valuable service where both people work and especialy if the household has young children.
